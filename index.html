Welcome to Arden Primary School

Arden Primary School is a school in which our pupils are supported to become considerate and responsible members of society. Arden Primary School Encourages our pupils to embrace the joys of learning and to challenge themselves every single day. Our aim is for parents and carers to work with us and to instil a learning ethos in their children. We pride ourselves on working together to make sure every child is given the opportunity to achieve their full potential because every child matters. We are directed by the wishes and choices of parents, carers, families and the wider community and place a value on all religions, traditions and backgrounds of our families and staff.
