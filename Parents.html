Parents

Regular attendance at school is important for children in order for them to reach their full potential.
Please ensure that your child arrives to school on time and please be on time to collect your child at the end of the school day.
If your child is unable to attend school please telephone school as soon as possible
Good manners and good behaviour are expected at all times and we appreciate your support in promoting this.

Forms for parents The following forms are available from the front office
1. Change of contact details
2. Permission to administer medication
3. Exceptional leave form
